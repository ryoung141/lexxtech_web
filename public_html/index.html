Services

Minor Service

This service is recommended every 5,000 miles, or every 6 months and includes:

  • Replace engine oil and genuine Toyota oil filter
  • Replace engine oil drain plug gasket
  • Rotate and balance tires, set tire pressure
  • Clean and adjust rear brakes and parking brake
  • Check and top off fluids
  • Perform 27-point visual inspection
  • Lube chassis if needed
  • Reset low tire pressure warning sensor
  • Reset Maintainence Required service light
  • Inspect and make adjustments on a per make and model basis

Intermediate Service

This service is recommended every 15,000 miles, or every year.

It includes everything from the Minor Service, in addition to:

  • Service and test battery
  • Replace engine air filter

Major Service

This service is recommended every 30,000 miles, or every two years.

It includes everything from the Intermediate Service, in addition to:

  • Replace cabin air filter
  • Replace differential oil

Extended Service

This service is recommended every 30,000, 60,000, and 90,000 miles.

It includes everything from the Major Service, in addition to:

  • Perform transmission service
  • Perform engine coolant service
